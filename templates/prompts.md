# Summarization Prompt

Summarize the following content. Keep it concise and produce:
1. A short (1-2 sentence) overview.
2. Key takeaways (3-6 bullet points).
3. Suggested study plan (3 steps).

# Question Generation Prompt

Create practice questions based on the content. Include:
- 5 multiple-choice questions (each with 4 options, indicate the correct option but keep separate)
- 5 short-answer questions.
- For each question, provide a short explanation.
